## Key Principles

✅ **Keep it simple** - Current foundation is solid, just enhance it  
✅ **Measure everything** - Analytics first, optimization second  
✅ **Avoid complexity** - Use proven tools, not custom builds  
✅ **Focus on funders** - Show traction, credibility, vision  
✅ **Iterate based on data** - Test, measure, improve

## What NOT to Do

❌ Build complex multi-agent AI before basics work  
❌ Create custom tools when proven solutions exist  
❌ Add features without validating demand  
❌ Neglect analytics and measurement  
❌ Overcomplicate database or architecture
